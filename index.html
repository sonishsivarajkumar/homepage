About Me

Hi, I'm Sonish Sivarajkumar and Sonish is an AI enthusiast and a Deep Learning researcher. I'm currently pursuing my Bachelor of Technology Degree from Government Engineering College,Thrissur under APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University , Kerala.

I'm doing my research on innovative applications of Machine Learning and have a research paper on inclusion of 'Automatic Power Factor Improvement based on Deep Learning' on Smart Meters for reduction of power consumption and better voltage stability . I'm an evolving innovator, teacher, learner and researcher.
