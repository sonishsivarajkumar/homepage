About Me

Hi, I'm Sonish Sivarajkumar , a Data Scientist and Deep Learning Researcher at Fractal Analytics . I have completed my Bachelor of Technology Degree from Government Engineering College,Thrissur under APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University , Kerala and have received Research Fellowship from IIT Madras in Deep Reinforcement Learning.

I'm doing my research on innovative applications of Machine Learning and have multiple research papers including 'Automatic Power Factor Improvement based on Deep Learning' on Smart Meters for reduction of power consumption and better voltage stability'. Also, I had been selected for the prestigious Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning programme jointly organised by IIT Madras , IIT Palakkad and ASAP Kerala.I have also been closely worked with Kerala Police Cyberdrome and has worked on their AI Team.

I'm a technical writer at Medium and my articles are revolved around Machine Learning. I'm Dean of School Of AI , a community to bring AI professionals and students together and was the Chairman of IEEE Computer Science Society at GEC Thrissur.

I'm an innovator, teacher, learner and researcher.
